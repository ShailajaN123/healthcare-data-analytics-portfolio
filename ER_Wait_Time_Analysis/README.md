# ER Wait Time Analysis

## Objective
Analyze emergency room wait times to identify peak periods, delays, and operational patterns that may impact patient experience.

## Tools Used
Power BI, Excel/CSV

## Dataset
The dataset includes emergency room wait time records with fields related to arrival time, wait time, urgency/triage level, and visit timing.

## Key Analysis
- Average wait time by triage or urgency level
- Wait time patterns by day and time
- Peak periods with higher patient delays
- Operational trends that may support staffing or workflow improvements

## Key Insights
- Wait times were higher during peak demand periods.
- Lower-priority cases generally experienced longer waits.
- Dashboard visuals helped identify when delays were most likely to occur.
